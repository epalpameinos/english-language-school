Our Philosophy

Welcome to the website of the Alexopoulou Foreign Language School, located in Patras, Greece and providing high quality English Language Studies for more than 30 years.

At our School, we cater to all ages and through targeted and personalized teaching, we meet the needs of all our students, whose progress we continually reward, motivating them to keep going strong, aiming for success!

We are committed to the successful outcome of the study of the English Language with respect for the personality of our students, providing a modern and comprehensive curriculum.

  • Excellent pedagogical methods of education
  • Cooperation with the best publishing houses
  • Flexible programs and specialized for every age of the student

With high-level teaching staff, we constantly monitor developments in the field of English Teaching, which is interactive and collaborative, student-centered.
